About Alli Kate

13 year old Australian Singer, Songwriter, Producer Alli Kate Bailey who has always had a love of music. She started playing piano at the young age of 2 years old, and progressed into writing and music composition while still in elementary/primary school. Over the following years she has evolved into an accomplished singer, songwriter with over 30 singles all available on all platforms. 


She writes pop music and doubles in some rap. Alli finds inspiration from artists like Tate McRae , Charlie Puth , The Kid LAROI , Ed Sheeran and even a bit of Adele. One of her singles “Not Anymore” which reached over 30k+ streams on Spotify in just a matter of weeks and growing, showing that people are interested in what she’s creating musically.


Alli has had hits that have reached over 500,000 streams across Spotify alone. In the last few weeks, Alli has blown up on Instagram gaining over 20k followers and having many videos, reach over 50 million views on instagram and counting!


Alli is proud of having her own sound, and loves the fact she can communicate through her music. All this and she’s not even yet a teenager. Alli is exited to see where her music will take her, and loves the fact that others enjoy her music as much as she enjoys creating it!

I just wanna thank all of y'all who support me can't believe what accomplished already and whats to come love y'all! - Alli
